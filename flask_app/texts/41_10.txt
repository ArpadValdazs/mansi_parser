Urin ekva erьɧ. Mortim ma siraŋ vitnьl, uj ta juvum. Man̩ tovьl rampьŋ sujil, sipьr maɧum unlene. Saŋkvli pilьŋ sāt jaŋkelma Nómtanumna ta patvesum. Am johtne ke man̩ porat man̩ aɧi, man̩ pьɧ vos on̩ꞩave. Ta sap voŋhan iseɧum, Polim katum toltьɧleɧum, polim laɧьlum toltьɧleɧum. Notьŋ aɧi vos on̩ꞩave, notьŋ pьɧ vos on̩ꞩave. nas erьɧ hun̩ likmi ta erɧave. Hun̩ urin ekva kasalave, ta erɧьltahtave.
