Parne pora – sāt ēt sɔχχa mań ɔtər ētt. I. Jik tūlne pora – konsəŋ ɔ̄jka, nuplane ɔ̄lne ētət. – Jēt laᵴɒlke jikawe χall-χall jikawe. ta jui palt ɔ̄s χall-χall: a) wōr mūχu̮m χūro̮m ēt. c) Šɔ̄χrəŋ ɔ̄jka – ak ēt. (ti pasəl jalpəŋı̮ɣ jimtr. ti pasəl ataı̮ɣ tara jikawe.) d) Paŋ ɔ̄jka – ak ēt. b) Urɒŋ χum – ak ēt. e) Nāj ēka (χal pāɣəlt ɔ̄li. kansəŋ ɔjk eka (?)). II. Jik kotˈiłˈ. Mań ɔtər ɔ̄u̯l ēt, mań ɔ̄tər nupəlam sāt ēt. III. satit ēt kit ētaı̮ɣ wārawe (jikawe). parne ēt tā. Akaı̮ɣ χūro̮m sāt. ta χalt χɔ̄tpa joχti, jir toti, ɔ̄s tau̯ pupɣe ak ēt jikɒŋke rɔ̄wi. tuu̯ pupɣe puplan ak ēt wuwe. χūrum pōɣəl koni pal: poral, χal jalpos.
