ńawram pȯ̭ŋke pupi̮ɣ͔ nupəl wārawe. mań ńawram mań kȯlnəl i̯anı̮ɣ͔ kȯln totawe, tuwəl pȯ̭ŋke pupı̮ɣ͔ nupəl wārawe. pupi̮ɣ͔ kasaꞩan ꞩirəl wārawe. pupi̮ɣ͔ kasaꞩanēte aman ūlum pattat ꞩāńen, āń ēkatēn susnsawe, man ńai̯t nēn tara=pattawe. tou̯ porat āń ēkatēn, tȯu̯ pȯrat ꞩāńēn man ꞩāꞩ ēkatēn ūlum pattat tara=pattawe, tuwəl ta pupi̮ɣ tȯri̮ɣ pȯ̭ŋke tuu̯ ta wārawe. ta pupi̮ɣ tȯri̮ɣ arsin tor taɣatawe. tau̯ i̯ani̮ɣmi, ɔ̄s i̯ir ali. akw l'apat χȯtpa ke sȯrŭmpati, ńawram pȯ̭ŋk=kostal ɔ̄lne porat, tuwəl pupɣēnəl mań parkan tars, man ɔ̄χsar sɔ̄u̯ lȯmt wiwe, tuwəl ńawram pȯ̭ŋken tuu̯ χanui̯aptawe. ti ńawram urɣalane māɣəs wārawe, usum χȯtpa ise ul wos i̯ȯχtəs.
