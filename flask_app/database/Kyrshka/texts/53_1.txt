Mojt: Man̩ mosne jaɧpiɧentl oleɧa. Hoʂa olseɧa. Vati olseɧa. toha olьmaten. Jaɧpiɧe jaɧaite nupil potьrtaŋke pats. Men homas akjot olьŋke patьmen əlale. Torom toha lavs: Jaɧaite jeɧpiɧe jot, olaŋke at-rovi. Potьrtь jaɧpiɧe os: nehotpa tav kinsi hum, a humhotpa kinsi ne. Tьhimaɧs menamen akjot olaŋkv at rovi. Jaɧaite jaɧpiɧe nupil potьrtaŋke pats. Men homs akjot olsmen elale olьmen. Jaɧpiɧe lavi ate men akjot at olьmen: Hunta elamholast olaŋke pateɧt, tan os toha pateɧtolaŋke. Jaɧpiɧin ne jaɧpiɧe palt minnaŋke patь, jaɧaɧьŋ hum jaɧaɧьte vuŋke pate mir telьɧlaŋke pateɧt. Jaɧpihe at hontlas joɧaɧite minas mat kaŋ. Taki maŋn hots kol vars.
